Salmon Recipe

How to make good salmon

Great tasting salmon for the family

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Chinese Cooking wine
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Place salmon in bag
  2. add in all ingedients (soy sauce, vinegar and cooking wine), mix and marinate in bag for 30 minutes minimum
  3. Cook salmon after marinating on skillet or air fryer
  4. Plate and serve with rice
